Healthcare UI/UX Case Study

Sahyog Healthcare App

Connecting users with essential medical services through intuitive design and seamless healthcare access

Category Healthcare Application
Client Sahyog Pvt. Ltd.
Project Date November 2023
Role UI/UX Designer

Project Overview

The Sahyog App is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to connect users with essential medical services, offering an intuitive interface for booking diagnostics, pathology tests, and doctor appointments.

The Challenge

Users needed a streamlined, accessible platform to book diagnostic tests, pathology services, and doctor appointments while ensuring security, transparency, and ease of use in healthcare service delivery.

The Solution

A user-centric healthcare application integrating secure authentication, comprehensive service selection, and streamlined booking processes for diagnostics, pathology, and medical consultations.

The Impact

Simplified healthcare access with efficient booking processes, enhanced user confidence through transparent pricing and secure authentication, and improved convenience in managing health services.

Key Features

Comprehensive healthcare functionalities designed for security and convenience

Login and Registration

Secure and straightforward authentication process ensuring user data privacy and account protection through multiple verification methods.

Phone Number Verification

Users can register and log in using their phone numbers with OTP verification process ensuring security and preventing unauthorized access.

Password Authentication

Secure password entry system to protect user accounts and maintain confidentiality of personal health information.

Service Selection

Easy navigation through various healthcare services offered by the app, providing users with comprehensive medical service options at their fingertips.

Diagnostic Services

Access to a wide range of diagnostic tests available from multiple accredited labs with transparent information.

Pathology Services

Comprehensive pathology testing options covering all essential medical examination requirements.

Doctor Appointments

Convenient booking system for scheduling appointments with qualified doctors through the app interface.

Diagnostics Booking

Comprehensive diagnostics section providing detailed information on available tests with transparent pricing and easy booking confirmation process.

Booking Confirmation

Design Process

A comprehensive, user-centered approach to healthcare application design

User Research & Personas

Conducted extensive user research to deeply understand the healthcare needs, pain points, and expectations of users seeking medical services. Developed detailed personas representing diverse user groups to guide the design process and ensure the solution addresses real-world healthcare challenges.

Wireframes & Prototyping

Created comprehensive wireframes to map out the app's structure, information architecture, and user flow for healthcare services. Developed high-fidelity prototypes to illustrate the app's functionality, demonstrating how users would navigate through diagnostics booking, service selection, and appointment scheduling.

Usability Testing

Performed extensive usability testing with potential users from various demographics to gather comprehensive feedback on the app's functionality and user experience. Iteratively refined the design to improve accessibility, ease of use, and trust factors based on user insights and testing results.

Visual Design

Focused on a clean and modern design aesthetic that instills trust and confidence in healthcare services. Implemented a consistent color scheme emphasizing health and wellness, with typography choices that enhance readability. Emphasized accessibility and ease of use throughout the design, ensuring all users can navigate healthcare services comfortably.
